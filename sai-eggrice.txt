scramble one or two eggs directly in small frying pan.
while eggs are cooking, microwave rice and frozen sausage for 1 min.
mix eggs up a bit. before hardening, put spinach on same pan. 
spray spinach with a mist of olive oil.
put egg on top of spinach to weigh down.  

once spinach is wilted down, transfer egg & spinach on to rice & sausage. 
pour some soy sauce.
